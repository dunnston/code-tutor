Early Game (Levels 1-5):

Power Strike (Level 1): Deal 150% weapon damage to a single enemy
Defend (Level 2): Increase defense by 50% for 2 turns
Quick Jab (Level 3): Deal 80% weapon damage with +20% crit chance
Minor Heal (Level 4): Restore 25% of max health
Taunt (Level 5): Force enemy to target you, gain +30% defense for 1 turn

Mid Game (Levels 6-12):

Whirlwind (Level 6): Deal 80% weapon damage to all enemies
Poison Strike (Level 7): Deal 100% weapon damage + apply poison (10 damage per turn for 3 turns)
Shield Bash (Level 8): Deal 120% weapon damage, 50% chance to stun for 1 turn
Mana Drain (Level 9): Deal 90% weapon damage, restore 20% of damage dealt as mana
Battle Cry (Level 10): Increase party's attack by 25% for 3 turns
Evasion (Level 11): Dodge the next 2 attacks
Cleave (Level 12): Deal 130% weapon damage to target and 50% to adjacent enemies

Late Game (Levels 13-20):

Execute (Level 13): Deal 300% weapon damage if enemy is below 30% health
Meteor Strike (Level 15): Deal heavy damage to all enemies, 2 turn cooldown
Paralysis (Level 16): 70% chance to stun target for 2 turns
Life Steal (Level 17): Deal 150% weapon damage, restore 50% of damage as health
Berserk (Level 18): Increase damage by 50%, decrease defense by 30% for 3 turns
Divine Shield (Level 19): Become invulnerable for 1 turn, 3 turn cooldown
Obliterate (Level 20): Deal 400% weapon damage to single target, costs 50% max mana
