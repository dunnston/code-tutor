# Level Two: The Depths Below

**Entry Point: The Descending Stairwell**

After defeating the Gelatinous Cube, you pass through ancient stone doors into a spiral staircase carved from bedrock. The air grows colder and damper as you descend. Strange phosphorescent fungi provide dim, eerie light. The mysterious glowing crystal from Level 1 pulses warmly in your pack, almost as if responding to something below.

At the bottom, you emerge into a vast underground complex. Four major passages stretch before you, each marked with ancient symbols:

---

## PATH A: The Flooded Catacombs (North Passage - Water Symbol)

**Starting Scene: The Antechamber of Tombs**
- **Description**: Water drips constantly from the ceiling. Ancient sarcophagi line the walls, many cracked open from within.
- **Immediate observation** (Intelligence check):
  - Success: Notice water damage patterns suggest periodic flooding from somewhere deeper
  - Failure: Just looks old and wet

**Initial Choice**:

### Option A1: Investigate the Opened Sarcophagi
- **First three tombs**: Already looted, bones scattered
- **Fourth tomb**: 
  - **Still sealed** with a puzzle lock (Intelligence check)
    - Success: Open to find 45 gold, ancient ceremonial mace (+3 damage, bonus vs undead)
    - Failure: Lock mechanism jams, can't try again
- **Fifth tomb**:
  - **Partially open**, something moved it from inside
  - **Combat**: 2 Skeletons emerge when approached
  - Victory: Find 30 gold, tarnished crown (worth 40 gold), warrior's shield (+3 defense)

### Option A2: Follow the Main Passage Deeper
→ Leads to **The Flooded Gallery** (below)

### Option A3: Investigate Side Alcove
- **Small chamber** with offerings altar
- **Charisma check** to offer respect to the dead:
  - Success: Feel spiritually protected (+10 HP, +1 to next three stat checks)
  - Failure: Nothing happens
- **Search altar**: Find 25 gold in offerings, old map fragment (piece 1 of 3)
- **Hidden passage** behind altar (Intelligence check to find):
  - Success: Opens to **The Bone Archives** (new area)

### The Flooded Gallery (from Path A main passage)

**Scene**: Long hallway, ankle-deep water throughout, water damage everywhere

- **Environmental Hazard**: Water makes Dexterity checks harder (-1 penalty)
- **Navigation**: Hall has multiple doorways along both sides

**Left Side Doors**:

1. **First Door - Memorial Chamber**
   - Statues of ancient warriors
   - **Combat**: 3 Skeletons activate when you enter
   - Victory: Search statues for hidden compartments
     - **Intelligence check**: Find 50 gold, enchanted gauntlets (+2 Strength checks)

2. **Second Door - Crypt Keeper's Office**
   - Desk with ancient ledgers
   - **Read the ledgers** (Intelligence check):
     - Success: Learn about "The Bone Lord" who rules the deepest tombs, mentions his weakness to holy items
     - Find notation: "Amulet of Warding stored in Archives vault"
   - Cabinet contains: 35 gold, healing potion x2
   - **Locked drawer** (Dexterity to pick or Strength to break):
     - Contains: Silver key (opens Archives vault), journal about underground lake

3. **Third Door - Collapsed**
   - **Strength check** to clear rubble:
     - Success: Leads to **The Bone Archives** (alternate entrance)
     - Failure: Too unstable, dangerous to try again

**Right Side Doors**:

1. **First Door - Flooded Storage**
   - **Waist-deep water** inside
   - **Dexterity check** to wade safely:
     - Success: Navigate without issue
     - Failure: Fall underwater, take 5 damage, lose 1 random consumable item
   - **Underwater search**:
     - Find: Waterlogged chest with 40 gold, rusty sword
     - **Combat**: 2 Rat Folk scavengers hiding in here
     - Victory: They have 25 gold, crude knives

2. **Second Door - The Offering Room**
   - Ancient shrine with candles somehow still burning
   - **Rest area**: Can recover 25 HP here
   - **If player prays/offers respect** (Charisma check):
     - Success: Blessed with "Light of the Depths" - glowing aura that reveals secret doors in catacombs
     - Failure: Respectful silence is enough
   - Donation box: 30 gold inside (player's choice whether to take it)

3. **Third Door - The Preparation Chamber**
   - Where bodies were prepared for burial
   - **Gruesome scene** but valuable items remain:
     - Embalming tools (can sell for 20 gold)
     - Preserved herbs (can make 1 healing potion)
   - **Cabinet search**: Find 45 gold, ceremonial dagger (+2 damage)
   - **Secret compartment** (Intelligence check with "Light of the Depths" bonus):
     - Holy water vial x3 (very effective vs undead)

**End of Gallery**: 
- Passage splits to **Underground Lake** (north) or **The Bone Archives** (east)

### The Bone Archives (multiple entry points)

**Scene**: Massive library carved into rock, shelves contain skulls and bones organized like books, some actual books remain

**Upon Entry**:
- Eerily quiet, scholarly atmosphere
- Thousands of skulls watch from shelves
- Study tables still have open books

**Main Areas**:

1. **The Reading Room**
   - Several intact books can be studied:
     - **Intelligence check** to understand ancient language:
       - Success: Learn fortress history, discover mention of "The Warden" who guards the prison below
       - Learn about crystal keys needed for Warden's Chambers
   - **Search the room**: 
     - Find 50 gold, spell scroll (single-use fireball attack)
     - Map fragment (piece 2 of 3) hidden in book

2. **The Curator's Station**
   - Central desk where archivist worked
   - **Still occupied**: 
     - **Combat**: 1 Skeleton Mage (casts magic attacks, harder fight)
     - Victory: Mage's staff (+2 Intelligence checks, can cast light), 60 gold
   - **Desk contains**:
     - Archives catalog (helps navigate other areas)
     - Blue crystal key (needed for Warden's area)
     - Letter mentioning "the dragon's true hoard lies past the prison, guarded by his most loyal servant"

3. **The Vault** (requires silver key from Crypt Keeper's Office)
   - Heavy locked door
   - **Inside**:
     - Amulet of Warding (+3 defense vs undead, +2 to Charisma vs undead)
     - 80 gold in collection boxes
     - Ancient spellbook (increases Intelligence permanently by +1)
     - Scroll of revealing (shows all hidden doors in current area)

4. **The Forbidden Section**
   - Sealed with warning signs
   - **Choice**: Respect warnings and leave OR investigate
   - **If investigate**:
     - **Intelligence check** to safely handle dangerous texts:
       - Success: Learn dark ritual that weakens the Bone Lord (boss fight becomes easier)
       - Find necromancer's notes worth keeping
       - Failure: Cursed by dark magic, -5 HP maximum until healed at shrine
   - **Trapped pedestal**: 
     - Contains powerful dark tome
     - **Dexterity check** to disarm:
       - Success: Take tome safely (can sell for 100 gold or keep for power boost)
       - Failure: Poison dart, 15 damage

**Exit Options**:
- Return to Flooded Gallery
- Secret passage (if found with Light of the Depths) to **Underground Lake**
- Stairs down to **The Prison Level** (lower)

  - Success: Catch blind cave fish (heals 10 HP when eaten)
  - Failure: Waste time, nothing caught

### The Island (center of Underground Lake)

**Approach**:
- Boat ride takes several minutes
- **Random encounter** during crossing:
  - **Dexterity check** to notice ripples:
    - Success: Spot them coming, prepared for combat
    - Failure: Surprised attack
  - **Combat**: 3 Rat Folk pirates on makeshift raft (trying to rob you)
    - Victory: 45 gold, better boat (faster/safer), pirate saber (+2 damage)

**The Island Itself**:
- Rocky outcrop with ancient mausoleum
- Entrance flanked by worn statues

**Mausoleum Entrance**:
- **Trapped door** (Intelligence check to detect):
  - Success: Disarm pressure plate
  - Failure: Darts shoot out, 12 damage
- **Heavy doors** require Strength check to open:
  - Success: Doors open with groan
  - Failure: Need to find another way OR try again at -1 penalty

**Inside - The First Lord's Tomb**:
- **Grand chamber** with sarcophagus on raised platform
- Murals show fortress's construction

**Combat**: 1 Skeleton Champion (tough mini-boss)
- Heavily armored, powerful attacks
- Guards his lord's tomb faithfully
- Victory: Champion's armor (heavy, +5 defense, -2 Dexterity), 75 gold, legendary blade (+4 damage)

**Sarcophagus Investigation**:
- **Charisma check** to respectfully open:
  - Success: Lord's spirit approves, grants blessing (+2 to all Strength checks permanently)
  - Failure: Open without blessing, still lootable but no bonus
- **Inside**:
  - 100 gold pieces
  - Crown of the First Lord (worth 150 gold OR can wear for +2 Charisma)
  - Map fragment (piece 3 of 3) - when combined, reveals secret treasure room location
  - Ancient sword (+5 damage, bonus vs evil creatures)

**Hidden Chamber** (Intelligence check to find trigger):
- Success: Reveals small alcove
- Contains: Red crystal key (for Warden's area), healing potion x3

**Exit**: Return via boat to shore

### Water Temple (from lake shore path)

**Scene**: Partially submerged temple dedicated to water deity, still maintains some magic

**Exterior**:
- Columns rising from shallow water
- Must wade through knee-deep water
- **Dexterity check** to navigate slippery stones:
  - Success: Reach temple dry
  - Failure: Fall in, soaked (-1 to Dexterity checks until dried)

**Temple Entrance Hall**:
- Mosaics show water rituals
- **Combat**: 2 Skeletons + 1 Skeleton Mage
  - Guarding temple from intruders
  - Victory: 55 gold, ritual components (worth 30 gold)

**Main Chamber**:
- Large pool in center, magically clean water
- Statues around pool
- **Healing pool**: Drink to restore full HP, removes curses
  - Can fill bottles: Create 3 healing potions (25 HP each)

**Investigation options**:

1. **Examine the Altar**
   - Still has power
   - **Charisma check** to receive blessing:
     - Success: Water breathing ability for next underwater section
     - Failure: Minor blessing only (+5 HP)
   - Offering box: 65 gold

2. **Search behind statues**
   - **Intelligence check**:
     - Success: Find hidden switches, one statue rotates revealing passage
   - Passage leads to **Priest's Chambers**

3. **Dive into the pool** (requires water breathing)
   - **Underwater tunnel** at bottom
   - Leads to **Underground River Tunnel**
   - Can find: Sunken offerings worth 45 gold, underwater cave with air pocket containing 50 gold and treasure chest

**Priest's Chambers**:
- Living quarters for temple priest
- **Well-preserved** due to magical protection
- **Loot**:
  - Priest's robes (+2 Charisma, +1 Intelligence)
  - Prayer book (teaches healing spell, single use per rest)
  - 70 gold in donations
  - Aquamarine gem (worth 60 gold)
- **Desk research** (Intelligence check):
  - Learn about deeper areas: "The dragon's servant, called The Warden, keeps prisoners who knew too much. Only the twin keys of crystal may pass his threshold."

**Exit Options**:
- Return to lake shore
- Underwater tunnel to Underground River
- Hidden passage to **The Prison Level**

### Underground River Tunnel (from lake or temple)

**Scene**: Natural cave system with rushing underground river, dangerous current

**Entry Point**:
- River flows rapidly through cavern
- Ancient bridge crosses it (partially collapsed)

**Crossing Challenge**:
1. **Use the broken bridge** (Dexterity check):
   - Success: Carefully cross damaged sections
   - Failure: Fall into river, swept downstream to **The Rapids** (dangerous area), take 15 damage

2. **Swim across** (Strength check):
   - Success: Make it across
   - Failure: Swept to The Rapids, take 10 damage

3. **Use rope and grappling hook** (if obtained from shore camp):
   - Automatic success

**Tunnel Exploration**:
- **Cave 1**: Rat Folk hideout
  - **Combat**: 4 Rat Folk bandits
  - Victory: Their stolen loot - 85 gold, various weapons, healing potion x2
  - Crudely drawn map showing "prison entrance through waterfall"

- **Cave 2**: Crystal formations
  - Beautiful glowing crystals
  - **Can harvest** (Dexterity check):
    - Success: Collect crystals worth 50 gold
    - Failure: Cut on sharp edges, 8 damage

- **Cave 3**: The Waterfall Cave
  - Large waterfall conceals passage
  - **Intelligence check** to find the path:
    - Success: Discover walkway behind waterfall
  - Leads to **The Prison Level** (back entrance)

**The Rapids** (if swept downstream):
- Dangerous fast-moving section
- **Strength check** to grab rocks and stop:
  - Success: Pull yourself out, find 30 gold wedged in rocks
  - Failure: Take additional 10 damage before washing up on beach
- Eventually deposits you at **Subterranean Beach** (new area)

### Cell Block C (from Guard Post)

**Scene**: Maximum security, reinforced everything, ominous atmosphere

**Entry**:
- Requires Guard Post keys
- **Heavy security door**
- Warning signs: "DANGEROUS PRISONERS"

**Guard Station** (inside):
- Better equipped than main post
- **Combat**: 2 Skeleton Guards (tougher variants)
  - Victory: 65 gold, master keys (open all C Block cells), chainmail (+4 defense)

**The Occupied Cell**:
- **Cell C1**: Only occupied cell
- **NPC**: Marcus (mentioned in journals) - still alive but aged, preserved by magic
  - **Charisma check** to earn trust:
    - Success: Marcus reveals crucial information:

**Other Cells**:

- **Cell C2**: Previous escape attempt
  - Tunnel partially dug (Strength check to finish)
  - Success: Opens to **Underground River Tunnel**
  - Find: Improvised tools, 30 gold

- **Cell C3**: Torture victim remains
  - Disturbing scene
  - Search: 45 gold, victim's diary describing Warden's transformation

- **Cell C4**: **Locked** even with master keys
  - **Dexterity check** to pick complex lock:
    - Success: Inside is prisoner who knew too much
    - Skeleton in chains
    - **Hidden in cell**: 80 gold, enchanted dagger (+3 damage, +1 Dexterity)

- **Cell C5**: Dark magic residue
  - **Intelligence check**: Recognize necromantic ritual was performed
  - Find: Dark ritual components (worth 50 gold), cursed skull (powerful but dangerous item)

**Solitary Confinement** (end of block):
- Pitch black, tiny cells
- **Requires light source**
- **Investigation**:
  - Cell S1: Prisoner scratched message in wall: "The dragon awakens when both keys turn as one"
  - Cell S2: **Combat**: 1 Ghost (magical enemy)
    - Difficult fight
    - Victory: Ghost's chains (magical item, +2 Strength)

**Exit Passage**:
- Leads to **The Warden's Wing** (locked without crystal keys)
- Alternative exit to **Interrogation Chambers**

### The Warden's Wing (Requires BOTH Crystal Keys)

**Entry**:
- Massive double doors with two keyholes
- Must have Blue and Red Crystal Keys
- Doors are ornate, magical energy humming
- **Insert both keys**: Doors slowly grind open

**The Approach Hall**:
- **Description**: Grand hallway, unnaturally cold, frost forming on walls
- Statues line path (9 total)
- **Trap**: Statues are actually constructs
  - **Intelligence check** to recognize:
    - Success: Notice magical auras, can avoid triggering
    - Failure: Step wrong, **Combat**: 3 Skeleton Warriors activate
- **Pass safely**: Find alcoves with offerings
  - 85 gold total, silver chalice (worth 60 gold)

**Antechamber**:
- Waiting room before Warden's office
- **Prisoner cages** hanging from ceiling (empty)
- **Desk**: Secretary's station
  - **Search**: 45 gold, schedule showing Warden's daily routine with dragon
  - **Hidden drawer** (Dexterity check): Spare key to Warden's personal quarters

**The Warden's Personal Quarters**:
- Living area (he never leaves this wing)
- **Investigation reveals**:
  - Half-human, half-monster transformation happening
  - Scales growing on furniture from his touch
  - **Loot**:
    - 150 gold in personal safe
    - Dragon scale fragment (evidence of transformation)
    - Better armor: Warden's coat (+4 defense, +1 Charisma)
  - **Diary on nightstand**:
    - "Day 200: The Master speaks clearer now. I understand my purpose."
    - "Day 350: The transformation hurts, but I will endure for the Master."
    - "Day 500: I am becoming something more. Something glorious."

**Shrine Room**:
- Small chamber where Warden worships dragon
- **Dragon altar** with offerings
- **Charisma check** to defile altar (weakens Warden in upcoming fight):
  - Success: Destroy altar, Warden will be enraged but weaker
  - Failure: Altar fights back magically, 20 damage
- **Loot**: 100 gold in offerings, dragon tooth amulet (+2 to all dragon-related encounters)

**The Warden's Office** - MINI-BOSS AREA:

**Scene**: Large office overlooking prison, desk covered in papers, window showing dragon's lair beyond

**COMBAT: The Warden (Mini-Boss)**
- **Description**: Partially transformed into dragon-human hybrid
- **Difficulty**: Tough fight, uses both weapons and magic
- **Abilities**: 
  - Breath weapon (minor dragon breath)
  - Heavy armor
  - Regeneration (heals slowly during fight)
- **If altar destroyed**: No regeneration, reduced damage
- **If read torturer's notes**: Know weak points, bonus damage

**VICTORY**:
- Warden drops:
